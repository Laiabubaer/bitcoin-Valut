1) Scope

This Policy applies to the Vault: Bitcoin Wallet application and informational pages on vaultbitcoin.org that are controlled by the Company. If you follow links to third-party services (e.g., explorers, fee APIs, fiat on/off-ramps), their privacy policies govern their processing. The Company does not receive data from those providers in ordinary app use.

2) Key Definitions

  • Self-custodial wallet: A wallet where only the user controls private keys and funds; the Company has no access.
  • Personal data: Any information that identifies or could reasonably identify a person.
  • Processing: Any operation performed on personal data (collecting, storing, using, disclosing, etc.).
  • Controller / business: Entity that determines the purposes and means of processing (when applicable).
  • Processor / service provider: Entity that processes data on behalf of a controller (not applicable to core app functions).

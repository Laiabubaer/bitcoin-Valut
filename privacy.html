Zero-Collection by Design

Vault: Bitcoin Wallet is a self-custodial Bitcoin wallet. We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal information. Private keys and wallet data remain on your device and are never transmitted to the Company.

Scope

This Policy applies to the Vault: Bitcoin Wallet mobile application and informational pages on vaultbitcoin.org that are controlled by the Company. If you follow links to third-party services (e.g., explorers, fee APIs, fiat on/off-ramps), their privacy policies govern their processing.

Key Definitions

  • Self-custodial wallet: A wallet where only the user controls private keys and funds; the Company has no access.
  • Personal data: Any information that identifies or could reasonably identify a person.
  • Processing: Any operation performed on personal data (collecting, storing, using, disclosing, etc.).

Self-Custodial Architecture

  • No custody: You exclusively control your private keys and funds.
  • On-device storage: Seed phrases, private keys, and wallet metadata remain locally on your device.
  • No transmission: The app avoids sending any personal data to our servers.
  • No recovery: We cannot access, restore, or reset your seed phrase or funds if lost.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Identifiers: name, email, phone, government ID, address.
  • Device/network data: device IDs, IP addresses, geolocation, user agents.
  • Usage/diagnostics: analytics, crash logs, session metrics.
  • Financial info: balances, transaction history, addresses.
  • Cookies in the app: none.

Technical Interactions

To broadcast transactions and fetch blockchain data, the app may connect directly to the Bitcoin network, to a node you operate, or to a node/provider you choose. These connections are user-controlled. We do not operate tracking endpoints.

Device Permissions

  • Camera (optional): scan QR codes. Images remain on-device.
  • Local storage: encrypted wallet data and backups stored locally.
  • Biometrics/Passcode (optional): unlock the wallet locally. Templates managed by the OS.
  • Network: communicate with the Bitcoin network or services you configure.

No Sale / No Sharing / No Ads

  • No “sale” or “sharing” of personal information.
  • No targeted advertising, analytics profiling, or data brokerage.

Security & Your Responsibilities

  • Back up your seed phrase offline.
  • Use strong device security.
  • Beware of phishing; we will never ask for your seed phrase.

Children’s Privacy

Vault: Bitcoin Wallet is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Data Transfers

Because we do not collect personal data in routine app use, there are no cross-border transfers for app operation. If you contact support, email infrastructure may route messages internationally.

Regional Disclosures

We follow international privacy frameworks (GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, PIPEDA, etc.). Since Vault does not collect data in normal use, these rights typically apply only if you email support. In that case, we will respect your rights under your local laws, including access, deletion, or correction.

Law Enforcement & Legal Requests

We maintain no user databases. If we receive a valid legal request, we can only provide information we actually possess (e.g., support emails). We will assess the validity of any request and object where appropriate.

Data Retention

  • App use: no data retained.
  • Support correspondence: retained only as long as needed to respond and comply with legal obligations, then deleted.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in the app, practices, or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on https://www.vaultbitcoin.org with a revised effective date.

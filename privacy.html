1) Scope

This Policy applies to the Vault: Bitcoin Wallet application and informational pages on vaultbitcoin.org that are controlled by the Company. If you follow links to third-party services (e.g., explorers, fee APIs, fiat on/off-ramps), their privacy policies govern their processing. The Company does not receive data from those providers in ordinary app use.

2) Key Definitions

  • Self-custodial wallet: A wallet where only the user controls private keys and funds; the Company has no access.
  • Personal data: Any information that identifies or could reasonably identify a person.
  • Processing: Any operation performed on personal data (collecting, storing, using, disclosing, etc.).
  • Controller / business: Entity that determines the purposes and means of processing (when applicable).
  • Processor / service provider: Entity that processes data on behalf of a controller (not applicable to core app functions).

3) Self-Custodial Architecture

  • No custody: You exclusively control your private keys and funds.
  • On-device storage: Seed phrases, private keys, and wallet metadata remain locally on your device.
  • No transmission: The app avoids sending any personal data to our servers.
  • No recovery: We cannot access, restore, or reset your seed phrase or funds if lost.

4) Data We Do Not Collect

  • Identifiers: name, email, phone, government ID, address.
  • Device/network data: device IDs, IP addresses, user agents, geolocation, mobile advertising IDs.
  • Usage/diagnostics: analytics, crash logs, session metrics, behavioral profiling.
  • Financial information: balances, transaction history, addresses—these are handled locally and/or by the Bitcoin network, not by us.
  • Cookies in the app: none.

5) Technical Interactions

To broadcast transactions and fetch blockchain data, the app may connect directly to the Bitcoin network, to a node you operate, or to a node/provider you choose. These connections are configured so that the Company does not receive personal data. If you enable optional third-party integrations (e.g., explorers, fee estimators, fiat on/off-ramps), those providers may process data under their own privacy notices.

6) Device Permissions

  • Camera (optional): to scan QR codes for addresses/PSBTs. Images are processed locally; not sent to the Company.
  • Local storage: to store encrypted wallet data and backups on your device.
  • Biometrics/Passcode (optional): to unlock the wallet locally. Biometric templates remain managed by the OS; not accessible to us.
  • Network: to communicate with the Bitcoin network or services you configure. We do not operate tracking endpoints.

7) Cookies & Website

The app sets no cookies. Our website (vaultbitcoin.org) is designed to operate without tracking cookies controlled by us. If this changes in the future, we will update this Policy and, where required, seek consent.

8) No Sale / No Sharing / No Ads

  • No “sale” or “sharing” of personal information (including cross-context behavioral advertising).
  • No targeted advertising, analytics profiling, or data brokerage.

9) Security & Your Responsibilities

  • Back up your seed phrase offline. Never share it with anyone, including us.
  • Use strong device security (passcode/biometric) and keep your OS updated.
  • Beware of phishing and social engineering. We will never ask for your seed or private keys.

10) Children’s Privacy

Vault: Bitcoin Wallet is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11) International Data Transfers

Because we do not collect personal data in routine app use, there are no ongoing cross-border transfers by the Company for app operation. If you contact support, email infrastructure may store your message in or outside your country depending on routing and service provider; we minimize retention (see Data Retention).

Our zero-collection design means we generally hold no user records to access, correct, delete, export, or share. If you voluntarily email support, we will process only what you provide to respond, and we will honor the rights and requirements applicable to that correspondence.
